Architecture & Methodology

This application utilizes a trained machine learning model to evaluate candidate profiles based on five quantitative metrics: Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA), professional internship experience, academic projects, standardized aptitude scores, and evaluated soft skills.

The model was trained on a comprehensive placement dataset sourced from Kaggle. You can review the original data and distributions here.
